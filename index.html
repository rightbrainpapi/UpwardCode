But it's not. Basketball is tough. However, basketball and other past-times provide a space for their practicioners to find enjoyement, challenge, and success which makes devloping both a love and mastery of the game worthwhile.

After-School

Upward Code offers After-school enrichment classes for grades K-8. During our 6 week project based program we teach our students web development concepts and through deconstructing sites then rebuilding them using the students own ideas.
